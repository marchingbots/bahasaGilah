ME

MEANING:
1. More formal
2. Implies the subject is the doer of the action.

Uses TRANSATIVE VERBS = the verb needs an object.


1. MENGE

Used with ONE-SYLLABLE verbs

Example: mengecat.

CHANGES:
None

2. MENG

8 Letters:
A, E, I, O, U (all the vowels)
G, H, K

Example: Mengambil
Menggambar.

CHANGES:
Mengirim - kirim (DROP THE K)

3. MEN

5 Letters:
C, D, J, T, Z

Example: Mencari
Mendapat.

CHANGES:
Menulis - tulis (DROP THE T)

4. MEM

4 Letters:
B, F, P, V

(when the lips come together) Example: membuat.

CHANGES:
Memilih - pilih (DROP THE P).

4. ME

8 Letters:
L, M, N, R, W, Y, Ng, Ny

Example: membuat.

CHANGES:
None
